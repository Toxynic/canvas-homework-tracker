Canvas Homework Tracker

Step 1 — Connect to your Canvas account

Use the exact Canvas domain (must include https://)
Token stays in your browser; revoke anytime in Canvas → Account → Settings.

How to get your token

  1. Open your school’s Canvas (e.g., your-school.instructure.com) and sign in.
  2. Go to Account → Settings.
  3. In Approved Integrations, click + New Access Token.
  4. Name it (e.g., “Homework Tracker”), set an expiration, then Generate Token.
  5. Copy the token and paste it above.

After connection: we’ll add Courses, Assignments, Today & Week views.
